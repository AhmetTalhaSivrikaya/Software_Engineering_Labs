## Actors
- User: Manages manual settings and views status.
- Sensors: Provide real-time data for occupancy and environment.
- Technical Team: Handles system configuration and performance monitoring.

## Key Functionalities
- Monitor Occupancy:Central use case (Included by Auto Adjustment).
- Safe Mode: An extension triggered during system faults or sensor errors.
- Energy Saving: Activated when no occupancy is detected.
