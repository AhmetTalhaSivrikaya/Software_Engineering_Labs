# Car Insurance Management System

A Use Case Diagram illustrating the digital interface between a customer and an insurance provider's database.

## Actors
Customer: The user seeking to manage their vehicle insurance policies.
Database:The external system storing policy details and transaction history.

## Use Cases
Login:** The entry point for all secure actions (Includes relationship with other services).
File a Claim: Allows users to report accidents or damages.
Renew Policy:Facilitates the extension of existing insurance contracts.
View Policy:Provides a read-only view of the current coverage.
Payment: Handles the financial transactions for renewals or new plans.

## Workflow Note
The diagram emphasizes that the Login use case is a prerequisite (Included) for the customer to access specific account related functionalities.
